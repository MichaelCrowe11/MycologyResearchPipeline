About Saved Searches

Saved searches allow you to store complex search queries for easy reuse. You can save searches for literature, compounds, or species analysis.

Benefits of saved searches:

  • Quickly rerun complex queries without having to recreate them
  • Track changes in research findings over time
  • Share search parameters with colleagues (premium feature)
  • Set up regular notifications for new results matching your criteria (premium feature)
